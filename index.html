About Me

Hi, I’m Shilpa, a Research Assistant and Customer Engineer with a master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence (cum laude) and years of experience in engineering and research. I have had the opportunity to work on real-world projects, which have significantly shaped my career.

My work has been recognized with the Pioneer Competitive Research Award, motivating me to continue pushing the boundaries of technology. One of the notable projects I worked on involved enhancing robot navigation and object handling. In this project, we developed a robot capable of moving inside a storage room, picking items from one location and placing them in another, while also comparing task performance between male and female operators to optimize the robot's efficiency.

In addition to this, I have worked on designing a user-friendly chat application with deep learning models and developed a real-time object detection system using YOLO on Raspberry Pi4. My technical skill set includes Python, Java, C/C++, and various AI/ML technologies, along with expertise in Hadoop, Spark, virtual machines, Docker, React.js, Django, and AWS.

Outside of work, I am passionate about leveraging my skills to address real-world challenges and contribute to innovative projects. If you’re interested in learning more about my work or exploring potential collaborations, feel free to get in touch!
